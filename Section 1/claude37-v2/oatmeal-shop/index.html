Frequently Asked Questions

Are your oatmeal products gluten-free?

Yes, all our oatmeal products are made with certified gluten-free oats. However, they are processed in a facility that also handles wheat products, so there may be traces of gluten.

How long does shipping usually take?

Standard shipping within Romania takes 2-3 business days. International shipping may take 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

What is the shelf life of your products?

Our oatmeal products have a shelf life of 6 months when stored in a cool, dry place. The exact expiration date is printed on each package.

Do you offer wholesale options?

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for bulk orders. Please contact our sales team at wholesale@oatmealdelights.com for more information.

How do I prepare your oatmeal products?

Simply add hot water or milk to the oatmeal, stir, and let it sit for 2-3 minutes. For a creamier texture, you can cook it on the stovetop for 5 minutes. Detailed instructions are on each package.
